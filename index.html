ASSOCIATE WORKER

Design and development

Our program is to create employment for unemployed workers. We will connect all workers through our app GETHELP, provide them ID in which they get ratings for their work. Our team will manage the presence of active worker. In free time worker can also communicate with each other and share their experience or train their colleague. We will collect some information's from worker for security and communication purpose. like phone number , email id , Addhar card etc.

PROVIDE WORK

We will provide work for workers from our app GETHELP Worker get the notification of their assigned work through GETHELP app and get the schedule from our customer. After communicating with the customer they get address by which we will reach them easily.

BENEFITS

Customer can demand according to their profession like a seller can demand a delivery boy , Driver etc. We will publish worker profession with their experience on GETHELP . In our day to life, we all need a person that can assist or help in our work to get it completed easily and fast. But it is not easy to find . So we ,will provide a platform where you can book your assistant as per your requirements .
